What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence (EI) or Emotional Quotient (EQ) is being aware of your own emotions and being able to identify and manage them. It is also about being aware of how those emotions affect those around you along with an awareness for how others are feeling.

The four attributes of emotional intelligence are:

  • Self-management
  • Self-awareness
  • Social awareness
  • Relationship management

How is it different to IQ?

Why is emotional intelligence important?

EQ is important as it allows you to communicate effectively and work productively with others. It also also allows you to adapt to change and challenges when they arise more easily.
